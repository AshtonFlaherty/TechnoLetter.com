Who am I and what do I do?

I’m Ashton Flaherty, a DJ and producer based in Virginia. My journey into dance music began at age ten, and over the last decade, I’ve transitioned from a casual listener to a dedicated student of the craft. What started as a childhood fascination has become a lifelong pursuit of the 'perfect track' a standard that continues to shift as I explore deeper into the underground.

Whether it's the raw energy of a 4AM Kru set or the technical precision of Cristian Varela, the goal of TechnoLetter is to bridge the gap between the listener and the booth. Music isn't just about sound; it's about the culture, the hardware, and the community that keeps the scene alive.
