Cristian Varela - Your Body Experience

[ MOUNTING ANALOG_MASTER ]

>> THE VIBE // PIONEER_STATUS

Cristian Varela is single-handedly one of the biggest underground pioneers of techno music in the late 90's into the early 2000's. Cristian continues to be more than a titan in the Dance music scene, having set the standard for today's music overall. From his heavy analog cuts released in 2003 to "Fascinator" released in 2010, Cristian never fails to keep the dance floor moving.

>> DEPLOYMENT // LIVE_CRAFT

With his impressive vinyl skills, constantly DJing vinyl-only sets—commonly with 4+ turntables—he is truly one of the biggest definitions of "setting the standard" with both DJing AND Producing.

Terry Hunter - Wonderful (Club Mix)

[ MOUNTING GOSPEL_HOUSE ]

>> THE VIBE // SOULFUL_GROOVE

Terry Hunter is another absolute powerhouse in the dance music scene. The Chicago-based producer is often referred to as "the legendary" for his deep roots and massive influence in this genre.

Clocking around 122-125bpm, it is widely celebrated and seen as embodying pure "Gospel-House" energy, blending spiritual lyrics with a driving, soulful dance floor groove. This song is notably cited as an instant classic that remains a peak time favorite in soulful house sets around the world.
